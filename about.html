Client Questions

You Ask, We Answer

The most common questions from clients before they start a project with us — answered honestly.

How much does a project cost?
Every project is scoped individually — no flat rates, no guesswork. Pricing depends on scope, timeline, and complexity. After a short discovery call we give you a clear, itemised quote with no surprises. Most brand identity projects start from $800 and web builds from $1,500.
How long does a typical project take?
A logo and brand identity typically takes 2–3 weeks. A full website is 4–8 weeks depending on pages and functionality. Rush timelines are available — just ask. We always set realistic deadlines and stick to them.
Do you work with international clients?
Absolutely. We work with clients across Nepal, Southeast Asia, Europe, and North America. Everything runs remotely — video calls, shared boards, and async updates keep communication tight no matter the time zone.
What do you need from me to get started?
Just a clear idea of what you're trying to achieve and who your audience is. We handle the rest — the research, the creative direction, the execution. You don't need to have all the answers before reaching out.
How many revisions are included?
All projects include two structured revision rounds — enough to get the work exactly right without scope creep. Additional rounds can be added at a flat rate. We find that two rounds is almost always enough when the brief is solid from the start.
